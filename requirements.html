These are the requirements to be eligible to travel to Mars!

  • Experience 3 G-Forces on our G-Force simulating device. This is needed to ensure that you will be able to withstand 3 G's during takeoff and won’t need immediate medical assistance as none will able to be given during this time.
  • Run 2 miles in under 25 minutes. This will be a test of your body’s ability to supply oxygen to your lungs as well as your overall physical fitness. There not always be a constant level of oxygen so your aerobic system will need to be robust.
