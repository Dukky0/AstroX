Safety

Safety is a big concern for those who are interested in this unique opportunity. We take it very seriously, and will be completely transparent about the measures we take to ensure your well being. Specifically made Mars Atmosphere Aptitude Chambers, or MAAC, are specially designed “space suits” to protect our customers from the lack of oxygen and an atmosphere on Mars. Similar technology, that creates a breathable environment by releasing oxygen, is used in all buildings and transport. Aboard the spacecraft, there will be a seat for every resident which will include the following safety measures: A harness, a breathing apparatus, and an emergency kit. In addition, there will be an extensive safety brief preluding takeoff that will cover every specific detail of the launch and landing process in addition to the safety measures that will be taken to ensure the safe return of all participants.
